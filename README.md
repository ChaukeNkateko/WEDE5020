# WEDE5020
Web development project
## Project Title
Jowi's Poultry Farm
## Brief history
Jowi’s  poultry farm was established in 2005, by a single mother wanting to create a legacy for her children. It started as a dream, which continues to strive in this competitive market. In the business’s early days, she only farmed broiler chickens in her home. As years passed and demand grew, she expended to farming layer chickens to also sell their eggs. With over 19 years of experience we can confidently say we know quality and strive for it . Jowi’s Poultry Farm produces only the finest poultry and eggs and that’s a fact 
## Mission statement
 To produces healthy and good quality poultry and eggs that meet the highest standard of food safety ,  while using good farming techniques, train the youth to make jobs in the community , support other women in farming
## Vision statement
 To be the most trusted source of healthy good quality poultry and fresh eggs and to help empower young in my community through modern sustainable farming ways 
•	Target audience
Business that stock in bulks e.g restaurants, local market and households 	
People who are looking for chicks to farm
 
## Website goal and objectives 
1.	The website must be able to generate leads, by acting as a online advertisement of the business and attract big businesses like hotels, restaurants and supermarkets
2.	This website must also increase online sales, by encouraging customers to buy products directly from their website from anywhere at any time and 
3.	it must promote new and popular items that are sold by the poultry farm makes checking out very easy, which will increase revenue through e-commerce
4.	The website must enable customers to order fresh poultry and eggs directly on the site without any problems 
The website must be able to provide information about the vaccination , show if the farm complies with the health standards and the show poultry certifications (free range, halal or organic)
•	Key performance indicators  
How many first-time users of the website come back to the website 
Measure the amount of users that 
o	Fill out enquiry forms 
o	Purchase any product from the poultry farm’s website  
Return on Investment (ROI): Measure revenue generated by the website vs. the costs of running and maintaining the website. If the cost of running the website is less than the revenue generated by sales from the website it can be a good indicator that the website is performing well 
Track online reviews, ratings, and mentions of the poultry farm if they are good reviews on the website, it shows how well the website is doing 
Track time each user spends on site, how many pages they scroll to per session, and the amount of social shar the website gets every month to check the website’s success
