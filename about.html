About us

Jowi's poultry farm was established in 2005, by a single mother wanting to create a legacy for her children . Jowi'poultry farm began as a dream and a way to make ends meet for her family. The poultry farm continues to strive in this competitive market.In the business early days ,we only farmed broiler chickens in my home . As the years went by and the demand growing, she expanded her business and started farming layer eggs and selling their eggs .with about 20 years of experience we can confidently say we know quality and strive for it .Jowi's poultry farm produces only the finest poultry and eggs and that's a fact!!

Mission statement

Our mission is to produce healthy and good quality poultry and eggs that meet the highest standard of food safety, while using good farming techiniques.we want to train the youth and make jobs inthe community reducing unemployment and offer support to other women im farming

Vision statement

we want to be the most trusted source of healthy, good quality poultry and fresh eggs in South Africa and we want to help empower young women in the community by teaching them modern sustainable farming ways
